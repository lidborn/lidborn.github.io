Kappetalize

A game jam game about getting rich by any means neccesary!

Introduction

Kappetalize is a game where 2-4 players compete against eachother to aquire as much money as possible before the time runs out. The more money you have in your moneybag the harder you will hit! But you will also move slower, making it harder for you to get back and deposit your hard earned money back to the bank. If you missbehave near a police officer he will chase you down and try to take back your hard earned money! The game is played locally with Xbox or Playstation controllers.

Development
